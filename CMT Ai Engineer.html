OVERVIEW

The AI Engineering Career Transition Mentorship Program is an intensive 12-week course designed to equip participants with comprehensive skills and knowledge in data engineering. Each week includes 6 hours of training sessions and 15 to 20 hours of self-learning through provided materials. Participants will undergo rigorous training and engage in hands-on projects covering a wide range of essential topics and tools, providing both theoretical knowledge and practical experience.

Journey

Your step-by-step learning path to mastering Data Engineeringâ€”combining theory, hands-on experience, and real-world capstone projects.

1
Week 1: Introduction to Machine Learning Algorithms

Exploring machine learning algorithms and their foundations through live training and 1-on-1 sessions, while implementing simple models to kickstart your career.

2
Week 2 : Introduction to AI and Cloud Platforms (AZURE & AWS)

Dive into AI basics and explore cloud platforms like Azure and AWS. Gain hands-on experience through practical exercises and kickstart your certification preparation.

3
Week 3: Data Handling on Cloud , Supervised Learning and Model Evaluation

Master supervised learning, model evaluation, and data handling on Azure and AWS, with hands-on exercises. Prepare for and complete the AI-900 and AZ-102 certification exams.

4
Week 4: Unsupervised Learning and Clustering

Explore unsupervised learning, dimensionality reduction, and model deployment on Azure, with hands-on practice using Databricks and Snowflake. Focus on AI-900 certification preparation.

5
Week 5: :Deep Learning and Neural Networks

Learn deep learning, neural networks, and CNNs with TensorFlow and Keras, while preparing for the AI-102 certification.

6
Week 6: Naturual Language Processing

Gain a solid understanding of NLP fundamentals and explore cloud-based NLP services. Enhance your skills with hands-on practical exercises.

7
Week 7: Computer Vision

Delve into computer vision concepts and explore cloud-based vision services. Refine your skills through hands-on practical exercises

8
Week 8: Robotics & AI Automation

Explore robotics and automation, with a focus on NLP and computer vision on the cloud. Gain practical insights into integrating these technologies for advanced solutions.

9
Week 9: AI System Design and Architecture on Cloud

Focus on AI system design, microservices, and containerization through hands-on practical exercises. Prepare for the Azure AI Engineer Associate (AI-102) certification exam.

10
Week 10: AI Ethics, Governance and Compliance

Examine AI ethics, governance, and compliance through in-depth discussions and practical exercises. Develop a strong understanding of ethical considerations in AI implementation.

11
Weeks 11,12: Capstone Project

Work on your Capstone Project, covering project selection, development, deployment, and presentation. Receive weekly 1-on-1 guidance and complete practical exercises, with an optional 3-week extension offering mock interviews and networking opportunities.

12
Week 12: Final Presentations, Certifications, and Career Transition

Present your final projects, complete certifications, and receive career transition support. Celebrate your achievements at the GIofAI Graduation Ceremony and take the final examination for certification awards.
