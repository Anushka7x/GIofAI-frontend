Who We Are

Our history, vision, mission
and current goals.

The genesis of the Global Institute of Artificial Intelligence (GIofAI) can be traced to its predecessor, the Open AI Research organization, which emerged in 2018. Evolving from community-oriented roots, it shifted to a more structured and sophisticated institutional framework, culminating in the establishment of GIofAI.

Purpose

The purpose of GIofAI is to advance and enhance society through the dissemination and application of knowledge in the field of artificial intelligence.

Mission

Our mission at GIofAI is to serve as the premier independent and trusted voice of AI innovation globally. We foster a community of leaders equipped to harness the power of AI for society's betterment. Through our initiatives, research, and educational programs, we build capabilities, promote ethical AI, and drive positive impact.
